Hear from our students!

Andrew (University of Pennsylvania, '23)

now Graduate Student, Princeton University

My GRAD mentor was far and away the greatest resource for everything on my graduate school application path. He walked me through all of the steps necessary to put together the most competitive application I could, starting more than a year in advance. I have not yet met anyone in graduate school who had as strong of a support system as I did in their application process.

Saarah (University of Pennsylvania, '22)

now Graduate Student, Northwestern University

Up until finding this program, I was completely in the dark about how graduate school applications worked, so GRAD effectively unlocked an entire career path for me. My Penn GRAD mentor, and several other participating graduate students, offered extremely valuable insight on my many essay drafts and my application as a whole. Also, in being surrounded by other students applying to graduate school, I was inspired and comforted throughout the application process. Thank you GRAD!!
